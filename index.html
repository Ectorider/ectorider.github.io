Race around the world to find the Ectomorphs

Ectorider is a fast paced action game mixing platformer, third person shooter and racing.

Inspired by Sonic, Klonoa and Trackmania, this game will make you travel across levels at full speed and challenge you by combining your platform and shooting skills.

Features

Electromagnetic Powers

Harness the power of a futuristic magnet to grab, throw, and smash enemies into each. Discover each enemies special ability and use them to fight, jump higher or gain speed.

Extreme Roller Movement

Dash, grind, and soar through levels on high-speed rollers. Chain tricks, wall-runs, and aerial stunts to keep your momentum flowing and your style sharp.

Unleash your creativity

Use each enemies sepcial ability to your advantage. Shoot, glide, bounce, and more. Your only limit is your own imagination!

A compelling story

Race around the world to save the Ectomorphs, find out Professor Ektos'evil plan and stop his robot army before it's too late.
